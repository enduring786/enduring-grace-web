PRINT ON DEMAND (POD)

We offer Print on Demand services with good quality t-shirts, prints and service.

HOW DOES PRINT ON DEMAND WORK WITH US?

1. Stock offered for POD:

  • Round Neck T-Shirts - Regular Unisex Fit - 13 colors in 6 sizes (S to 3XL)
  • Round Neck T-Shirts - Oversized Unisex Fit - Black and White

Fabric of the above mentioned t-shirts will be : 180 GSM, Full Biowash, Pure Cotton, Compacted

2. Printing:

Printing is done using High Quality DTF (Direct to Film) Printing Method with proper double fusing techniques. This gives a better feel and longer life to the prints.

Once you onboard with us for Print On Demand service.

1. A special document will be maintained between you and Enduring Grace. This document will contain the following details:

  • Order Details like T-Shirt Color, Size, Print to be done
  • Customer Details like address, phone number
  • Fusing Status
  • Dispatch Status
  • Shipping Status
  • etc

2. To place orders under Print On Demand service, you will have to e-mail us the orders containing the following details:

a. T-Shirt Color & Size

b. Print File in the proper required format

3. Shipping:

The required details for shipping will be obtained from the document maintained between you and Enduring Grace.

Once the t-shirts are ready, will ship them to your customer. We ship All Over India.

Alternatively, you can book the shipments using different shipping partners and send us the shipping label. This will let you have control over logistics and its costs.

For Pricing and further details, you can Contact Us.
