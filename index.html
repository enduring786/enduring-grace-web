How Do We Customize

How do we customize?

DTF (Direct to Film)
PRINTING

We offer customization via High Quality DTF Printing. These serves customers:

  • Who wish to print complicated and color rich designs,
  • Who need to print lesser quantities,
  • If the designs are intricate and need more precision.

The printing is done at high settings using good quality sheets and inks. Personal attention is given at the time of printing.

SCREEN PRINTING

Screen Printing Method is relatively used since a long time in the customization business. We offer Good Quality Screen Printing Customisation Service for customers who have:

  • Simple designs with limited color combinations,
  • More quantities per design,
  • Needs economy of price.

We do not compromise on the paints or fusing methods due to which the feel, life and after wash results of the print are satisfactory.

EMBROIDERY

Embroidery is a common form of customization that we offer to a lot of our clients. It is subjective and preferred by customers:

  • Who wish to have a more premium look in their t-shirts,
  • The designs are usually logos and not very complicated,
  • The quantities are reasonable enough to obtain a decent price.

We offer embroidery service majorly to our corporates clients and they are satisfied with it since we do it using high end automated machines.

STITCHING

We also customize via stitching or manufacturing process when the client has a specific requirement that can be done only by making the t-shirts from scratch.

To be able to process the orders for manufacturing process, a lot of factors are considered like:

  • Order quantities
  • Manufacturing and Delivery Timelines.
  • Any specific requirement that can only be done on unstitched tshirts.
  • Any specific fabric requirement which is not available in ready made t-shirts.

Hence, we are able to satisfy our clients since we have the option of manufacturing which gives flexibility and more scope of creativity.
